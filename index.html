Cornell Data Science
I'm a part of Cornell Data Science's deep learning team, which explores the development and application of neural networks. Neural networks are a type of machine learning algorithms loosely based off of how our own brains work, with many "neurons" communicating and producing a response given an input. I'm currently working on implementing convolutional and recurrent neural networks and I plan on completing a project to generate original music with deep learning in the near future.
